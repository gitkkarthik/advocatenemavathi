About Me

Professional Legal Advocate Since 1998

I am Advocate S. Nemavathi, practicing in Tiruchirappalli High Court for 28+ years, handling cases independently with a result-oriented approach, both professionally and ethically.

As a Central Government Notary Public and Central Government Standing Counsel, I bring authoritative legal expertise. Additionally, as a High Court of Chennai certified mediator, I specialize in resolving disputes amicably in pre-litigation stages.
